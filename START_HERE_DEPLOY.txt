│ 🔧 HOW TO IMPROVE:                                                      │
│                                                                         │
│ 1. Replace the generic statement with a specific action or moment      │

│ 3. Include concrete details: Who did you help? What did you do?       │
│ 4. Quantify your impact if possible (numbers, results, outcomes)      │
│ 5. Focus on your unique approach or perspective                       │

│ learning each person's dietary preferences and favorite conversation   │
│ topics. Mrs. Chen taught me about her life in Taiwan; Mr. Rodriguez   │

│ highlight of everyone's week, including mine."                         │

Color Coding:
  🔴 RED    = Urgent fix needed (clichés, weak verbs, major issues)
  🟡 YELLOW = Can be improved (vague statements, minor issues)
  🟢 GREEN  = Strength (lean into this, expand on it)

Category Icons:
  🔴 Red    → fa-exclamation-triangle (warning)
  🟡 Yellow → fa-exclamation-circle (alert)
  🟢 Green  → fa-check-circle (success)

Feedback Icons:
  ❓ fa-question-circle → WHY
  🔧 fa-wrench → HOW
  💡 fa-lightbulb → SUGGESTION

Animation:
  • Flash effect when scrolling to card (1s)
  • Smooth scroll behavior
  • Hover effects on cards
