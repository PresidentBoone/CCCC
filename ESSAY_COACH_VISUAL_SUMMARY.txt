🎯 KEY FEATURES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. DETAILED FEEDBACK CARDS
   • Category label (Cliché, Weak Verb, Vague, etc.)
   • Why section - explains the problem
   • How section - 2-3 actionable steps
   • Suggestion - concrete alternative

2. NON-RANDOM HIGHLIGHTING
   • AI selects meaningful sections
   • Focuses on clichés, weak language, vague statements
   • 5-10 strategic highlights per essay
   • Balanced red/yellow/green mix

3. INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE
   • Click any highlight in essay text
   • Automatically scrolls to feedback card
   • Flash animation draws attention
   • Seamless text-to-guidance connection

4. VISUAL HIERARCHY
   • 🔴 RED: Serious issues (clichés, weak language)
   • 🟡 YELLOW: Opportunities for improvement
   • 🟢 GREEN: Strengths to celebrate
